Flourish & Blotts

A great place to be stranded

The Flourish & Blotts is a Library and a book store that has a plethora of books to quench any avid reader's thirst.Knowledge is power and books hold key to knowing. Established in 1777 by Sir Felix Flourish and Sir Brubeck Blotts as a public library, the library soon expanded to a bookstore in 1889 and in 2003 got a digital section that contains CDs and now a virtual music station.F and B also includes a cafe for book lovers to read their books peacefully. A tourist spot in itself because of its old Gothic architecture, F and B attracts a million people round theb year. It has been known to sell books in price that is even less than one-sixth of the actual worth. Visit us and know why there is always so much of a fuss about us.
